Mammals

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. Lions are social animals that live in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their large ears, trunks, and tusks.

Birds

Eagle

Eagles are large birds of prey known for their powerful beaks and keen eyesight. They are often seen as symbols of freedom.

Parrot

Parrots are known for their colorful feathers and ability to mimic human speech. They are highly intelligent and social birds.

Reptiles

Snake

Snakes are legless reptiles that use their muscles to slither across the ground. They have a unique method of hunting and feeding.

Turtle

Turtles are known for their hard shells and slow movement. They can live both in water and on land.
